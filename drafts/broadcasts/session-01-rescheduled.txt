Scope: developing a historiography of American technical capital.

We have to work through the technical character of capital in order to define
its conditions of reproduction. We need to understand how capital can be
reproduced from without, rather than claim that capital can be seized without
such apprehension.

That task is usually waived away. For this first session, we will take
inventory of the categories we have inherited and mark where new ones have to be
invented.

The entrypoint for forking capital is software. Software is the liaison between
superstructure and base. But that does not mean becoming 'hackers' without
history.

Marx Compute Club is a build club for the infrastructure of theoretical
production.
