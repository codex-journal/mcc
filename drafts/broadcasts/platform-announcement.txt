Marx Compute Club is opening its first infrastructure surfaces.

The website and announcement list stay at:

https://www.marxcompute.club/

MCC ID is the identity surface. It uses passkeys through Pocket ID. The point is
not to collect passwords. The point is to keep source, chat, and future
infrastructure from becoming separate identity fragments.

https://id.marxcompute.club/

Marx Compute Source is the source surface. It is a self-hosted Forgejo instance
for MCC repositories, issue trails, patches, and contribution metadata. Access is
review controlled. Public read access and mirrors will open where appropriate.
Arbitrary signup and arbitrary write access are not enabled.

https://git.marxcompute.club/

MCChat is the chat surface. It is IRC through a web client, not Discord and not a
closed social platform. Access is invite controlled for now, and the web client
is the supported path at launch. The immediate goal is a working room for source,
theory, simulations, and operational traces.

https://chat.marxcompute.club/

This is the first pass at MCC as source infrastructure: a build club for the
infrastructure of theoretical production. The platform is part of the work. It
will be revised in public where that makes sense, and kept review-controlled
where that is the honest operational boundary.

name or handle you want attached to the request and a sentence about what you
want to work on or follow.
